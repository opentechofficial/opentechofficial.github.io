Neo OS

Dat Compatible Browser

The Browser that comes with Neo OS will automatically pass through the Tor network (as well as all internet communications), it will be geared towards security with an ad block, and all scripts will be disabled. The default search engine will be duckduckgo, and it will warn users of tracking from websites such as google or facebook.
